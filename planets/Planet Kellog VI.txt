Kellog 6, Quarter deck, is a maximum security Prison World; it holds many hundreds of thousands of convicts. Because the inmates refine fuel as part of their rehabilitation you should find very low fuel prices on Quarter deck. Financial reports also indicate that Quarter deck pays reasonable bounties for captured criminals. Processing is handled at the Justice Star satellite just outside the planet. The black market reportedly is high, Captain for luxury goods - narcotics especially. Life must be hard for those men and women. Any item offering them an escape would probably fetch a good price.
